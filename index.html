OUR STORY

THE ORIGIN

The product that didn't exist — so we built it.

St. Pete Tallow doesn't start in a laboratory. It starts on a volleyball court — on the white sand beaches of St. Petersburg, Florida, under a sun that doesn't negotiate.

Our founder spent his days exactly like his customers do: outside, active, alive. Playing beach volleyball, building community, logging hours in salt air and Gulf heat that most skincare products were never designed to survive.

He was using whatever was on the drugstore shelf — water-based lotions that evaporated before noon, leaving skin dry, cracked, and peeling by the end of the workday. Then a coworker handed him a homemade tallow balm. He was skeptical. He tried it anyway.

It worked. Better than anything he'd ever used. But it was heavy, greasy, and waxy. The right ingredient in the wrong formula.

So he did what founders do: he didn't go looking for a better product. He built one — engineered for the community he'd already spent a year building on the sand.
